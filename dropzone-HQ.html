Dropzone HQ


A web-based solution for skydiving dropzone management.

Learn More

The Concept

Share Information Faster

Currently, many dropzones track instructorsâ€™ jumps and parachute repacking on paper. Dropzone HQ seeks to reduce the human error and slowdown caused by this system, giving employees quicker, more accurate access to the information they need.


Manage Records

Dropzone HQ will manage general inventory, gear rentals, gear location, and the gear repair process, reducing the hassle and innacurracy of paper records.


Provide Insight

As a bonus to managers and owners, various statistics will be gathered as Dropzone HQ is used, sharing valuable insight on the dropzone's workflow.

The Tech

Dropzone HQ will be a web application with mobile support, built with:

ReactJS & Bootstrap frontend
MySQL & Django backend
Amazon Web Services hosting
