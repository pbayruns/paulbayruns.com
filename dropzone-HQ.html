Dropzone HQ


A web-based solution for skydiving dropzone management.

Learn More

The Concept

Currently, many dropzones keep paper records to track instructorsâ€™ jumps and parachute repacking. Dropzone HQ seeks to reduce the human error caused by this system while also allowing employees to more quickly view and edit the information they need. On the equipment side, Dropzone HQ will also manage general inventory, gear rentals, gear location, and the gear repair process. As an added bonus, various statistics will be gathered as the system is used, giving valuable insight to owners and managers.

The Tech

Dropzone HQ will be a web application with mobile support, built in ReactJS. With its backend based in SQL and Django, DZHQ will provide real time views of data that is critical to the hectic workflow of a busy dropzone. Powered by Amazon Web Services hosting, DZHQ will be scalable to dropzones of various sizes.
