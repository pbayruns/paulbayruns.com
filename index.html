Projects

Steam Achievements Viewer

A visualization of steam achievements that utilizes Valve's API to access and display achievement data.

BathroomFinder

A website that displays a google maps view of all bathrooms on Rowan's Glassboro campus, among other features. Includes support for signing in, rating bathrooms, and leaving comments.

Made possible by Bootstrap, MySQL, PHP, and JQuery.

Dropzone HQ

A web application for management of employees, inventory, and repairs at skydiving dropzones. In development as my undergraduate senior project.

Built with ReactJS, MySQL, AWS, and Django.
